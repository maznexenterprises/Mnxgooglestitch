What are your core financial service areas?

expand_more
Maznex specializes in sovereign wealth management, cross-border investment architecture, and elite corporate financial restructuring. Our experts operate at the intersection of high-growth markets and established financial hubs.

How does the recruitment fee structure work?

expand_more
Our executive search services operate on a premium retained model, ensuring dedicated resources for sourcing the top 1% of global talent. Performance-based incentives are structured according to the seniority and strategic impact of the placement.

Do you provide multi-currency billing?

expand_more
Yes. As a global authority, we facilitate billing and settlements in all major reserve currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, AED, SGD) to provide seamless financial integration for our international clientele.

How can I book a 1-on-1 consultation with a partner?

expand_more
Qualified inquiries can request a strategic brief through our "Book a Free Call" portal. Upon vetting, you will be assigned a Regional Partner based on your geographic requirements and service vertical.

What global compliance standards do you follow?

expand_more
We adhere to the highest international standards, including FATF guidelines, GDPR for data integrity, and specific regional regulatory requirements in all jurisdictions we operate in (FCA, DFSA, MAS).
